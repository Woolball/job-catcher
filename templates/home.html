How it works

Job search is time-consuming and stressful. With Job Catcher I want to save time by focusing on highly relevant job matches. Here's how:

Job Ad Retrieval

Collect ads from various platforms, using Google's job search API.

Semantic Matching

Match your CV with job ads, using an SBERT model, and inspired by Susan et al. 2024.

Traditional Matching

Perform keyword and skill matching to ensure no important terms are missed.

Scoring & Ranking

Combine the results into a final score, ranking your best job matches.
