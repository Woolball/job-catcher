How it works

Job search online is time-consuming, stressful, and full of irrelevant results. With Job Catcher I want to save time by focusing on highly relevant job matches. Here's how:

Job Ad Retrieval

We collect job ads from various job boards and platforms, using the JSearch API.

Semantic Matching

We analyze the meaning of your CV and job ads for accurate matches, using an SBERT LLM.

Traditional Matching

We also perform keyword and skill matching to ensure no important terms are missed.

Scoring & Ranking

The results from all methods are combined into a final score, ranking the best job matches for you.

Tips for Best Search Results

Make sure to get as many jobs as possible:

  • Enter several job titles to broaden your search.
  • Include variants of the same role. Consider adding the seniority level.
  • Don't worry about getting some irrelevant results at this stage.

Examples:

  • Project Manager, Senior Project Manager, Program Manager, Project Coordinator
  • Qualitative Researcher, Research Analyst, User Researcher, Market Research Analyst, Qualitative Data Analyst

Help us know exactly what you're looking for:

  • Enter as many skills as needed to reflect your unique profile.
  • Include skills you excel at and want to see in your dream job.
  • Upload your CV for semantic matching with job descriptions.

Examples:

  • Agile methodologies, risk management, budgeting, MS Project, team leadership, conflict resolution, Prince2, resource allocation, change management, vendor management
  • Interviews, ethnographic research, thematic analysis, NVivo, research design, content analysis, grounded theory, narrative analysis, qualitative data visualization
